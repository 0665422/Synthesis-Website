Trump Impeachment

On November 15, 2019 a transcript of phone call between United States President Donald Trump and Ukraine President Volodymyr Zelensky was released. It revealed highly controversial contents of the phone call. Whistleblower stated that Trump violated his oath and tried to use his position in office for personal gain. Impeachment process started.

Low-Level Two-Sentence Thesis

Although both articles talk about the phone call between President Trump and the President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelensky both have a different take on if what was said qualifies as impeachable offenses. The New York Times article insists President Trump should be impeached by congress while the New York Post article brushes off the situation and urges that we shouldn’t overreact. Both articles helped us understand one crucial idea and it’s that we should look at this situation through unbiased eyes, wait for all the facts and what the hearings being held will reveal. If he is found guilty it’s a huge deal, knowing that the President of one of the most powerful countries in the world tried to leverage crucial government aid in exchange for information on a presidential candidate’s affairs should be impeachable.

Synthesis Question

Is Ukraine-Gate something we should brush off or is it and impeachable offense that should be agreed on by the Congress?
